Order-to-Cash Cycle Analysis

An interactive breakdown of workflow improvements.

Project Overview

Many businesses face challenges with visibility and efficiency in their order-to-cash cycle, leading to delays and missed revenue opportunities. This analysis showcases a redesigned workflow that addresses these bottlenecks. By mapping the current process and introducing parallel tasks and automation, the new system aims to significantly speed up the invoicing cycle and reduce errors. Explore the comparison below to see the impact of these changes.

Current State: Bottlenecks & Delays

The current process is prone to delays due to sequential, manual handoffs and a lack of visibility between departments. Each team must wait for the previous one to complete its tasks, creating significant idle time.

Future State: Streamlined Workflow

The redesigned process utilizes parallel actions to speed up the cycle. As soon as an order is placed, both Fulfillment and Finance are triggered, eliminating wait times and accelerating the entire process.
